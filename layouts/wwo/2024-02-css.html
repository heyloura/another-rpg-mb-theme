I don't need CSS

HeyLoura: A Standard's Rebel
Weird Web October
2024 Submissions

This is an ode to a time before.

A time when the HTML standard itself allowed you to style your webpage.
You were not burdened with adding a style-sheet and table layouts reigned supreme.
Now such whimsy disappears.

Others are warned away from this path: ignore the deprecations at your own peril.

third

The height of excellence

The humble marquee tag

100% proven to distract your website visitors

As browsers, with their march forward, leave behind such treasures things.
One day, dear visitor
You will visit this page
The colors will be gone and the blinking text will be still

And the DVD logo will never hit the corner.
