This is an ode to a time before.

A time when the HTML standard itself allowed you to style your webpage.
You were not burdened with adding a style-sheet and table layouts reigned supreme.
Now such whimsy disappears.

Others are warned away from this path

As browsers, with their march forward, leave behind such treasures as these.
One day, dear visitor
You will visit this page
The colors will be gone and the bouncing text will be still
